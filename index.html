Mac AutoScroll Test

Instructions:

  1. Make sure the extension is loaded in Chrome
  2. Click the middle mouse button (scroll wheel) anywhere on this page
  3. Move the mouse to scroll in different directions
  4. Click again to stop scrolling

This is a large scrollable area to test the Mac AutoScroll extension.

The content extends far beyond the viewport in both horizontal and vertical directions.

Test Section 2 - Center Right

This section is positioned to the right to test horizontal scrolling.

Try middle-clicking and moving your mouse left and right.

Test Section 3 - Bottom

This section is positioned far down to test vertical scrolling.

Try middle-clicking and moving your mouse up and down.

Test Section 4 - Far Bottom Right

This section tests diagonal scrolling capabilities.
